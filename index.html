    🍚 Ghanaian Jollof Rice

    How to make it:

    1. Blend tomatoes, onions, peppers, and garlic into a sauce.
    2. Fry the sauce with oil until it thickens and smells rich.
    3. Add rice, seasoning, stock, and a little curry powder.
    4. Simmer gently until the rice is cooked and flavorful.
    5. Serve with chicken, plantains, or salad.
